Your Weekly Master Plan

A strategic overview for your crop's entire lifecycle, from seed to harvest.

Week 1: Germination

Focus on consistent moisture. Protect seedlings from cutworms and damping-off disease.

Week 2: Seedling Care

Apply a light starter fertilizer. Scout for early pests like aphids and flea beetles.

Week 3: Vegetative Growth

Begin regular, deep watering. Apply first round of nitrogen-rich fertilizer.

Week 4: Canopy Development

Monitor for leaf diseases like early blight. Ensure good air circulation.

Week 5: Nutrient Boost

Apply micronutrients (Zinc, Boron) based on soil test. Continue scouting for pests.

Week 6: Pre-Flowering

Ensure soil has adequate phosphorus and potassium. Prepare for flowering stage support.

Week 7: Flowering Stage

Crucial watering period. Avoid nutrient stress. Monitor for flower-dropping issues.

Week 8: Pollination & Fruit Set

Protect pollinators. Scout for fruit borers. Apply calcium to prevent rot.

Week 9: Fruit Development

Maintain consistent watering. Apply a balanced NPK fertilizer. Watch for stink bugs.

Week 10: Sizing Up

Ensure potassium levels are optimal for fruit/grain size. Manage canopy for sunlight.

Week 11: Maturation

Reduce nitrogen application. Scout for late-season diseases like powdery mildew.

Week 12: Ripening

Reduce watering frequency to increase sugar content and improve quality.

Week 13: Harvest Prep

Prepare storage and equipment. Monitor for pre-harvest pests and rots.

Week 14: Harvest Window

Harvest at peak maturity based on visual cues and weather forecasts for best quality.

Recommended Daily Tasks

Simple, actionable steps to take every day to ensure your plan's success.

Watering & Moisture

Check soil moisture 2 inches deep. Water early in the morning if dry, avoiding leaf wetness to prevent fungal growth.

Scouting & Monitoring

Walk your fields. Look for signs of pests (under leaves), diseases (spots), and nutrient deficiencies (yellowing).

Weather Check

Review the next 24-48 hour forecast. Plan sprays or irrigation based on predicted rain, wind, and temperature changes.
